# Integration by parts

Proof:
Start with product rule:

$\frac{d}{dx} f*g=f'g+fg'$

Integrate both sides:

$f*g=\int f' g dx+\int f g' dx$
$\int f' g dx=f*g-\int f g' dx$

define $u=f'$ and and $v=g$

$\int u v dx=(\int u dx)  v- \int (\int u dx) * v' dx$
