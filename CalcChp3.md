# Limits at infinity

n is the largest exponent

$$\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{a_1 x^n+ a_2 x^{n-1}+\cdots}{b_1 x^n+b_2x^{n-1}+ \cdots}
$$
$$=\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{a_1+a_2 x^{-1} + \cdots}{b_1 + b_2 x^{-1} \cdots}$$

$$=\frac{a_1}{b_1}$$
